Employee Profile Model

Part of the Knowledge Engine: Identity and Access Layer

Model Overview

The CustomUser model extends Django’s AbstractUser and is used to manage employees and technicians in the datacenter. It includes authentication information, personnel details, shift tracking, and certification history.
